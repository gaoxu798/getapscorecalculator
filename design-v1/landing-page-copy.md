## 7. FAQ｜购买异议

**Q1: Is this free to use?**
A: Yes. All 38 AP score calculators are free. No signup, no credit card, no limits on how many times you use it.

**Q2: Is this an official College Board tool?**
A: No. AP® and Advanced Placement® are registered trademarks of College Board. This is an independent tool based on publicly available scoring guidelines. Your actual score is determined solely by College Board.

**Q3: How accurate is the prediction?**
A: We use the latest official scoring rubrics and composite score tables. However, College Board adjusts cutoffs slightly each year based on exam difficulty. Treat the result as a strong estimate, not a guarantee.

**Q4: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. The free calculator works instantly in your browser. Pro features (saving history, weakness analysis) require an account so we can store your data.

**Q5: Is my data stored or shared?**
A: Free version: nothing is uploaded. All calculations happen in your browser. Pro version: calculation history is saved to your account. We do not sell data. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

**Q6: Can I get a refund if I upgrade to Pro?**
A: Yes. All paid plans have a 14-day refund window. Email jordivh9@gmail.com to request a refund. Processing takes 5–10 business days.

**Q7: How is this different from Fiveable?**
A: Fiveable is a full AP prep platform. We do one thing: score calculation. That means faster load times, a cleaner interface, and no login required for the free version.

**Q8: Which subjects are covered?**
A: All 38 AP subjects, including Calculus AB/BC, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, US History, World History, English Language, English Literature, Psychology, and more. [See full list](/subjects).
