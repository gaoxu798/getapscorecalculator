# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** May 9, 2026

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. We use cookies to provide, protect, and improve our Service.

## Cookies We Use

| Category | Cookies | Purpose | Duration |
|----------|---------|---------|----------|
| Essential | `__vercel_jwt`, session cookies | Site functionality, security | Session / Persistent |
| Analytics | `_ga`, `_gid`, `_gat` (Google Analytics) | Traffic analysis, page optimization | 26 months |
| Performance | Sentry-related | Error monitoring, stability | 90 days |

## Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

## Analytics Cookies

We use **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve the Service.

- **What we track**: Page views, time on site, referral sources, device type
- **What we do NOT track**: Your AP scores, inputs, or calculation history
- **Opt-out**: Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## Your Choices

### EU/UK Users
Under GDPR/ePrivacy rules, we ask for your **consent** before loading non-essential cookies (Analytics). You can:
- **Accept all** cookies
- **Reject non-essential** cookies (Analytics will not load)
- **Manage preferences** via the cookie banner

### All Users
You can disable cookies in your browser settings:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-4617-2e355526790e)

**Note**: Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
